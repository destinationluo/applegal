Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 03, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ProTempo ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Summary: We respect your privacy. ProTempo is designed to process your golf swing data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data on our servers.

1. Data Collection and Usage

A. Personal Data
We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

B. Sensor Data (Apple Watch & iPhone)
The App uses motion sensors (accelerometer and gyroscope) on your device to analyze your golf swing rhythm. This data is processed in real-time locally on your device. We do not transmit this raw sensor data to any external servers.

C. HealthKit Data
If you grant permission, ProTempo may interact with Apple HealthKit to read or write workout data. This data is used solely to track your practice sessions locally. We do not sell or share your HealthKit data with third parties for advertising purposes.

2. In-App Purchases

Our App offers a "Lifetime Pro" purchase option. All financial transactions are processed directly by Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your credit card information or billing details.

3. Third-Party Services
