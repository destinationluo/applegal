1. Information We Collect

Crucial for Functionality: To provide accurate Solunar forecasts, sunrise/sunset times, and weather data, our application requires access to your device's geographical location.

How it is used: Your location coordinates are processed locally on your device or sent to our weather data providers solely to retrieve the relevant forecast for your area. We do not track, store, or share your historical location history for marketing purposes.

We may collect non-personal information about how the Service is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This Usage Data may include information such as your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address), browser type, device model, operating system version, and other diagnostic data.
