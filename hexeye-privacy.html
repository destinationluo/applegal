Privacy Policy for Hex Eye

Effective Date: January 3, 2026

1. Overview

Hex Eye ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. We believe that your data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy describes how Hex Eye handles your data.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal information, usage data, or file contents. We have no servers and no user accounts.

3. Local Processing

All file operations (viewing, editing, analyzing, and saving) are performed locally on your iOS/iPadOS device using Apple's native APIs. Your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to any external server or cloud service by Hex Eye.

4. File Access

Hex Eye only accesses the specific files you explicitly choose to open via the iOS Files picker (UIDocumentPicker). We do not scan your device or access other files without your permission.

5. Third-Party Services

Hex Eye does not use any third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase) or advertising networks.
