Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 21, 2026

1. The Short Version

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data or server credentials. NodeGuard is built on a Zero-Cloud philosophy. What happens on your device, stays on your device (or in your private Apple iCloud).

2. Server Credentials & Data Storage

All server IPs, usernames, passwords, SSH keys, and API tokens are stored securely in your device's native Apple Keychain. If you enable sync, this data is synchronized using your personal iCloud account. We have zero access to your servers, your logs, or your credentials.

3. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use third-party analytics trackers (like Google Analytics or Firebase) within the app. We do not track how many servers you have or how often you restart them. If the app crashes, crash logs are only sent to us if you explicitly opt-in via Apple's native iOS crash reporting system.

4. Changes to This Policy

If we ever decide to change this policy (which is highly unlikely given our zero-cloud architecture), we will update this page. Your continued use of the App implies your agreement to the current Privacy Policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@parentinglab.app
