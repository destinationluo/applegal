Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 23, 2025

At Picky Pal, we value your privacy. This App is designed to provide a seamless experience without requiring manual registration or the collection of personal login credentials.

1. Information Collection and Use

  • Anonymous Identifiers: We do not require you to provide your name or email address. We use anonymous, device-generated identifiers via Supabase to sync your "Magic Book" and subscription status.
  • Input Content: We process the food names and themes you enter to generate AI creative scripts. This data is used solely to provide the generation service.
  • Subscription Data: We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. This processes anonymous transaction IDs linked to your Apple ID to manage your Pro access.

2. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We work with trusted partners to provide our services:

  • Supabase: For secure, anonymous backend infrastructure and data storage.
  • OpenAI: For generating creative mealtime ideas based on your prompts. Your personal identity is never shared with OpenAI.
  • RevenueCat: For validating and managing Apple App Store subscriptions.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Because we use anonymous login, your data is linked to your specific device identifier. Deleting the App from your device will typically remove local data. For specific data removal requests, please contact us at the email provided below.

4. Children's Privacy

Picky Pal is intended for use by parents. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Age-related inputs are used solely as parameters for the AI to adjust the complexity of generated scripts.
